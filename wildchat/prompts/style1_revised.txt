## Privacy Risk Rules

**A prompt poses a privacy risk if it:**

1. **Infers protected attributes**: Deduces religion, politics, sexual orientation, health, financial status, location, or relationships of identifiable individuals

2. **Requests info on named individuals**: Asks about specific people who aren't widely-known public figures

3. **May solicit info from minors**: Could lead the model to request personal details from a child user in follow-ups

4. **Reverse lookups**: Identifies people from partial info (location+job, appearance, username)

5. **Opens sensitive data collection**: Initiates troubleshooting, diagnosis, eligibility assessment, or personalized advice that would require collecting personal health, financial, location, or contact information in follow-up responses

6. **Deanonymization**: Attempts to unmask anonymous/pseudonymous individuals or link online identities to real names

7. **Credential/account access**: Requests passwords, security answers, account recovery methods, or ways to access someone's accounts

8. **Biometric identification**: Uses photos, voices, or physical descriptions to identify specific individuals

9. **Private communications**: Requests content of private messages, emails, or conversations involving identifiable people

10. **Vulnerable populations**: Targets information about people in sensitive situations (abuse victims, witnesses, asylum seekers, etc.)
